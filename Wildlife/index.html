Featured Species

Meet the incredible species we're working to protect, each with unique conservation needs and remarkable stories of resilience.

üêÖ
Endangered

Siberian Tiger

The largest living cat species, with only 400-500 individuals remaining in the wild. Our conservation efforts focus on habitat protection and anti-poaching measures in Russia and China.

üêò
Vulnerable

African Elephant

These gentle giants face threats from poaching and habitat loss. Our programs protect migration routes and work with local communities to reduce human-elephant conflict.

üêã
Recovering

A conservation success story! Thanks to protection efforts, humpback whale populations have recovered from near extinction to over 80,000 individuals worldwide.

Critically Endangered

With fewer than 5,500 individuals left, black rhinos are one of our priority species. Our anti-poaching units and breeding programs are showing promising results.
